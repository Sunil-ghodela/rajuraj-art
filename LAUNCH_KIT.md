## 2. WhatsApp Business setup

Download "WhatsApp Business" from Play Store (separate from regular WhatsApp). Use Raju's number if he's primary, or your number.

### Business profile

- **Business name**: RajuRaj art
- **Category**: Art & Entertainment → Artist
- **Description**:
```
Handcrafted Himalayan miniature landscapes. Made in New Tehri by Raju Raj Manav.
Made one at a time. Shipped across India.
```
- **Address**: New Tehri, Uttarakhand (optional — leave blank if you prefer)
- **Email**: optional
- **Website**: your Vercel URL (e.g. `rajurajart.vercel.app`)

### Auto-reply: "Welcome message"

Set it to fire when someone messages first time.

```
Namaste 🙏

Thank you for reaching out to RajuRaj art.

Each piece is made one at a time by Raju Raj Manav, from moss, paint, and the memory of the Himalayas.

Tell us:
1. Which piece you want (name + price)
2. Your city / pincode for shipping

We'll respond within a few hours and share UPI for payment.

— RajuRaj art
New Tehri, Uttarakhand
```

### Auto-reply: "Away message"

For nights / when you can't respond immediately.

```
Hi! We've received your message and will reply within 12 hours.

In the meantime, you can browse all pieces here:
[your Vercel URL]

— RajuRaj art
```

### Quick replies (set up under Business Tools → Quick Replies)

These let you type `/keyword` to insert a full message.

- **`/upi`** →
  ```
  Please pay via UPI to:

  UPI ID: [YOUR-UPI-ID@bank]
  Amount: ₹[AMOUNT including shipping]

  After payment, share the screenshot here. We'll confirm and ship within 2 days.
  ```

- **`/shipping`** →
  ```
  Shipping is ₹150–300 across India based on your pincode. International orders — message us first.

  Each piece is hand-packed: bubble wrap + cardboard + fragile-marked. Arrives in 5–8 days.
  ```

- **`/process`** →
  ```
  Each piece is handmade from moss, paint, wood base, and varnish. Raju makes one at a time, takes 7–10 days per piece.

  No two are exactly alike — each one is unique.
  ```

- **`/custom`** →
  ```
  Yes, custom orders are possible! Tell us:
  - Size you want (small / medium / village-style)
  - Any specific elements (peaks count, houses, plants)
  - Your budget range

  Raju will make a one-of-a-kind piece for you. Takes 10–14 days.
  ```

### Catalog setup

Under Business Tools → Catalog → Add 6 items.

For each piece, use the photo + name + price from the website. Customers can browse the catalog inside WhatsApp directly.

His work deserves to be seen.

Made him a small site. Take a look. Order one if it calls. Or just share with someone who'd appreciate.

[your Vercel URL]

#supportlocalartists #handmadeindia
```

Post this on:
- Your personal Instagram (story + grid post)
- Personal WhatsApp status
- Facebook (if you use it)
- Any community groups (Uttarakhand groups, art collectors, expats abroad who'd love a piece of home)

The goal of week 1 isn't sales — it's **visibility and signal**. Sales follow naturally.

```

Decide at end of week 4 whether to scale (more pieces, paid domain, paid ads, marketplace listings) or pause/iterate.

---

## 5. What we are NOT doing (yet)

- ❌ Paid ads — too early, no signal yet
- ❌ Etsy / marketplace listings — wait for first 10 orders + reviews
- ❌ International shipping setup — handle case-by-case via WhatsApp first
- ❌ Custom branded packaging — bubble wrap + a hand-signed note is enough for v1
- ❌ Razorpay / Stripe — UPI direct is faster, simpler, no fees

These all become useful **after** Week 4 review, if there's traction.
