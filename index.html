The artist

Raju Raj Manav

40 years. From New Tehri, Uttarakhand.

Handcrafts Himalayan miniature landscapes — snow peaks, moss slopes, mountain villages — from memory of the hills he was born in. Each piece is shaped from moss, paint, wood, and patience.

One at a time. No two are the same.
